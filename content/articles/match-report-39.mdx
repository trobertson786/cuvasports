## Match Report

BRENTFORD beat AFC Bournemouth 4-1 at the GTech Community Stadium yesterday afternoon in front of 17,152 fans thanks to a hat-trick by Kevin Schade with goals in the 7th, 51st and 90th+6 minutes and an own goal by goalkeeper Djordje Petrovic in the 39th minute which sealed a good home win for the Bees against the Cherries. Bournemouth pulled a goal back through Antoine Semeyno in the 75th but it was never enough.

Brentford came into the match with one win against Wolverhampton Wanderers (away) 2-0, one draw Leeds United (home) 1-1 and three losses Manchester City Carabao Cup (away) 0-2, Tottenham Hotspur (away) 0-2 and Arsenal (away) 0-2. Meanwhile AFC Bournemouth came into the game with three draws Burnley (home) 1-1, Manchester United (away) 4-4 and Chelsea (home) 0-0 and two losses against Everton (home) 0-1 and Sunderland (away) 2-3.

Brentford are unbeaten in their last nine league games against Bournemouth (won seven, drawn two), including all six meetings in the Premier League (won four, drawn two).

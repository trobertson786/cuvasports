## Match Report

LINCOLN CITY welcomed Chelsea to the LNER Stadium on Tuesday night in the Carabao Cup 3rd round in front of 10124 fans and gave the World Champions a first half fright as Rob Street put the home side ahead in the 42nd minute with a right-footed shot from an assist by Ivan Varfolomeev to make it 1-0 which was the half time score. But Chelsea came back strongly at the beginning of the second half to win 2-1 through two great goals to take the lead in the 48th minute through a brilliant right-footed shot from Tyrique George front where to make it 1-1. Then two minutes later Chelsea came forward to score a quick second goal through Facundo Buonanotte with a close-range right-footed shot from an assist by Tyrique George to make it 1-2 in the 50th minute and that was the way it stayed as Chelsea went into the hat for Round 4 after a tough Lincolnshire trip.

Lincoln City manager Michael Skubala had a big week ahead Chelsea won the World Champions in the City of Lincoln. The Imps won against Luton Town 3-1 on Saturday and were well up for the 3rd Round Cup tie against a weakened Chelsea line up. They were beaten 3-1 in Munich and 2-1 at Old Trafford against Bayern Munich and Manchester United last time out.

Lincoln City kicked off the first half and Chelsea struggled to contain the early pressure of Lincoln City who threw the kitchen sink at Chelsea early in the game.

Lincoln City had a third first half corner in the 31st minute. Chelsea’s back four valued at £186m did not win a tussle against the League 1 side. Chelsea kicked off the second half well and certainly had stern words from boss Enzo Maresca the coach as Chelsea came out of the blocks strongly scoring two great goals to take the lead in the 48th minute through a brilliant right-footed shot from Tyrique George front to make it 1-1.

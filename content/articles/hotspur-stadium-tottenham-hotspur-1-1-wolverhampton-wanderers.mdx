## Match Report

TOTTENHAM HOTSPUR could only manage a 1-1 draw with Wolverhampton Wanderers at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium last night – the home side missed an opportunity to go 2nd in the Premier League behind Liverpool and above Crystal Palace after failing to beat a plucky Wolverhampton Wanderers who scored first through Santiago Buerno in the 534th minute with a close range shot after Tottenham failed to clear a corner.
